Overview

Context Flow is a Chrome browser extension that helps users organize their browsing experience by automatically categorizing tabs into intelligent workspaces. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

üîí Privacy-First Design

Context Flow operates entirely on your local device. We do NOT send any data to external servers, track your browsing, or share information with third parties.

Data Minimization Principle

We collect only the minimum data necessary for Context Flow to function:

  • No browsing history - We don't store your complete browsing history
  • No personal information - We don't collect names, emails, or personal details
  • No form data - We don't access passwords, credit cards, or form inputs
  • No cookies - We don't read or store website cookies
  • No downloads - We don't access your download history
  • No bookmarks - We don't read your saved bookmarks

‚ùå What We DON'T Collect

  • We do NOT send any data to external servers
  • We do NOT track your browsing across websites
  • We do NOT collect personal information like passwords or payment details
  • We do NOT share data with third parties
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking services

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Automatically categorize tabs based on website content and context
  • Organize your browsing sessions into workspaces
  • Save your notes and preferences
  • Provide intelligent suggestions for workspace organization

Data Storage & Security

How We Secure Your Data

Your web history data is processed with the highest security standards:

  • Local Processing Only: All data analysis happens on your device
  • Chrome's Security: Data is encrypted using Chrome's built-in security mechanisms
  • No Network Access: Extension never connects to external servers for data processing
  • Profile Isolation: Data is tied to your specific Chrome profile only

Data Retention Policy

  • Automatic Cleanup: Old tab data is automatically removed when workspaces reach the maximum tab limit (default: 20 tabs)
  • User Control: You can delete any workspace and its data at any time
  • Complete Removal: Uninstalling the extension immediately removes ALL stored data
  • No Backup: We don't create backups of your data anywhere

Technical Security Measures

  • Content Security Policy: Strict CSP prevents code injection attacks
  • Manifest V3: Uses latest Chrome security standards
  • Minimal Permissions: Only requests permissions absolutely necessary for functionality
  • No External Dependencies: No third-party libraries that could compromise security

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all stored data through the extension interface
  • Modify: Edit or delete workspaces and notes at any time
  • Delete: Remove all data by uninstalling the extension
  • Export: Data can be viewed in Chrome's developer tools if needed

Third-Party Services

Context Flow does NOT use any third-party services, analytics, or external APIs. The extension operates entirely offline using only Chrome's built-in APIs.

Children's Privacy

Context Flow does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension is designed for general productivity use.

Compliance

This extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • Chrome Extension Manifest V3 requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) guidelines
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
